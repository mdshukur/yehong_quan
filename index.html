Edradour 10-Year-Old Single Malt

Handmade, using production methods that have been virtually unchanged in the last 150 years, Edradour 10 years old is a sherry bomb. Aged for a decade in a combination of Oloroso sherry & bourbon casks, this single malt is well balanced. On the nose, it is roasted nuts, dried fruits, cranberries, fruit-cake, sea salt and sugar almonds. On the palate, delicious honey notes are followed by fruity custard, hints of toffee, apple strudel and balanced with toasted oak

Bowmore 12-Year-old Islay Single Malt

Bowmore 12yo has a place in many hearts as Islay's 'medium-peated' malt. A pronounced iodine character with plenty of pepper, the current 12yo bottling is a welcome return to the classic Bowmores of the 1960s and '70s, with the emphasis on tropical fruit and smoke. A deserved runner-up in our Whisky of the Year 2014-15 blind tasting. Nose - Subtle lemon and honey, balanced beautifully by Bowmore's trademark peaty smokiness. Palate - Sweet and delicious heather honey and gentle peat smoke. Finish - The long, mellow finish.

Laphroaig 10-Year-Old Islay Single Malt

This Laphroaig has been distilled the same way today as when it was invented 0ver 75 years ago. Malted barley is dried over a peat fire formed from peat only found on Islay. Matured in American oak ex-bourbon barrels for no less than ten years developing hints of seaweed, sweet smoky malted barley and a bold, rich flavour. Nose: Huge smoke, seaweedy, “medicinal”, with a hint of sweetness. Taste: Surprising sweetness with hints of salt and layers of peatiness. Finish: Lingering.
